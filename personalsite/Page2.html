My Future

Time For A Change of Scenery

I have always been interested in computer programming. Since I was young computers just made sense to me. I was the IT helper around the house, and I learned everything by exploring. The limitless nature of computers has always fascinated me. But my knowledge is not as complete as I would like it to be, but I wanted to change careers and this seemed like a great step. I had the opportunity to attend in San Francisco, the tech capitol of the world, so I took it.

I would definitely consider myself a beginner in terms of coding or programming. And at this point I am not sure where I would like to specialize in the field. I was running social media for a restaraunt group in Boulder, taking photos and posting, doing some analytics but it was basically all user based. I wanted to see what was happening behind the website. I want to become more than just a user. I hope to land a job as a web developer, but I am open to whoever wants to take me in.

Some ideas would be for a DAW company like Ableton or Propellerhead, but those specific companies are based in europe so that might not be the most realistic idea. I have a huge interest in music, and the industry has become much more digitalized and available to the average joe. I am also a big sports nerd, so using my development skills to work with sports analytics or fantasy sites would be interesting as well. At the very least I will know something I did not know before, and be able to use that in my small business idea of managing social media departments for small businesses or something along those lines.

I started as a bus boy at a restaurant in Boulder with no knowledge of how to run a restaurant, and worked my way up to the top. I feel I am right back to the bottom again. I am daunted, nervous and excited. Life is too short just to get by, and this is my way of taking a leap of faith.
